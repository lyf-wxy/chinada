The First Session of CHINADA International Anti-Doping Symposium will be held in Beijing

The First Session of CHINADA International Anti-Doping Symposium will be held on 16th-17th July 2019 at the Beijing International Convention Center, Beijing, by CHINADA together with WADA. The first symposium will focus on Implementing Effective Anti-Doping Program in Major Events, and CHINADA will share the successful experiences of anti-doping programs in major events with ADOs all over the world.

We are greatly honored to have invited Sir. Craig Reedie, the President of WADA; Mr. Benjamin Cohen, the Director General of ITA; Mr. Hayashi Kazuhiro，the Director of Asia/Oceania of WADA；Mr. Ulrich Hass，the Chair of Code Drafting Team from WADA and Ms. Hirai Chika, the Director of Doping Control of Tokyo 2020 to attend the symposium. They will share the latest policies and development in anti-doping around the world, as well as the progress of anti-doping programs in Tokyo 2020 and Beijing 2022.

CHINADA is dedicated to preparing for the symposium, which is valued highly and strongly supported by the General Administration of Sport of China. In order to strengthen the cooperation in global anti-doping program, CHINADA will hold the International Symposium every year to provide a professional information exchange platform to pave the way towards all anti-doping organizations worldwide developing together hand in hand.
